“Pig!”
Raskolnikov laughed as though he could not restrain himself. So laughing,
they entered Porfiry Petrovitch’s flat. This is what Raskolnikov wanted: from
within they could be heard laughing as they came in, still guffawing in the
passage.
“Not a word here or I’ll... brain you!” Razumihin whispered furiously, seizing
Raskolnikov by the shoulder.
